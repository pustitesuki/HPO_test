What You Can Do

01
Recognise emotional manipulation
Disinformation is designed to trigger strong reactions. Pause before sharing — ask yourself why this content makes you feel what it does.
02
Evaluate sources critically
Check the original source, look at publication dates, cross-reference with established outlets, and verify image origins.
03
Report disinformation
Use platform reporting tools and specialist organisations. Flagging helps analysts and reduces the spread of harmful content.
